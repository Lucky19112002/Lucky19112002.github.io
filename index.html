High Resistant Fire Fighting Robot

We have developed a cutting-edge prototype of a robot capable of withstanding high levels of heat resistance. Its primary function is to enter burning buildings and effectively extinguish fires using a standard firefighting water sprayer. Additionally, we have incorporated advanced facial recognition software and a sophisticated detection system that can identify humans and assess the oxygen levels within the buildings, determining whether they are dangerously low or not.

Shell Share

Our project focuses on addressing the challenges of cyber security, particularly in relation to time-consuming manual testing within companies. Currently, the entire workload is placed on employees. To alleviate this burden, we have developed a tool called SHELL SHARE. This innovative solution allows users to collaborate and form a network, enabling them to collectively identify and address bugs in the system. Whenever a user discovers a bug, it is promptly registered in the logs, and all other network members are immediately informed and updated about the bug that was found.
